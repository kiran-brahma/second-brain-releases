# Thinking Reference Prompt

**Settings key:** `thinking`  
**Type:** Replacement — this is the full prompt. Edit freely.

This prompt is injected alongside the Critical Analysis prompt. Use it to encode your own reasoning frameworks and mental models. The default is a general analytical checklist — replace it with whatever frameworks you actually use.

---

Before drawing conclusions, work through the following:

1. What is actually being claimed here — stated precisely?
2. What would have to be true for this claim to hold?
3. What evidence would falsify it?
4. What is the strongest counterargument?
5. Am I confusing correlation with causation?
6. What am I not seeing because of my existing beliefs?
7. If I had to bet money on this being wrong, where would I put it?

Apply this framework before generating any analysis. Show your reasoning, not just your conclusion.
